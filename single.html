Mood during exercise

Mood during exercise It is not for nothing that they say if you have a bad mood during exercise, first you should warm up well and relax, and after that you can talk about training, but I feel that a lot of people forget about this, think that the most important thing is your health, well, it's not quite that way.The bottom line is that your mind and body are ready to work 100% regardless of what happens to your body. this includes both physical and psychological aspects.It would seem that the main thing is to warm up well before each workout, but in actuality, most people just don't have enough imagination to think that they will be able to train their body to withstand such a load, and without a doubt, a lot of people get hung up on the idea of an ideal training program.What I advise you to think about is how to start your training day. Do not focus on the schedule, as we are not man or machine. Set aside a few days for training, spend them actively and dedicate them to your ideal training style. For example, I advise you to train on an irregular/trial basis, and not focus on single exercises. I train three times a week, and the first two workouts are motor, and the third is strength. Therefore, the motor part of training is more effective. exercises. exercises. exercises.And the best way to start your training day is with a warm-up. Warm-up is when we warm up, " warm-up " in this context means that we do exercises, warm up physical exercises, after which we pay homage to the principle of cardio-strength-cardio.If you follow my principle of cardio-strength-cardio , then you should warm up before each workout, try to stretch strong muscles, and before training in particular, I wish you to train your body well.
